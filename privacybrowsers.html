I'm really glad that as a last second decision I added bits because it shows a pattern. There is a very strong positive correlation between bits of info and uniqueness.
To see how much bits affect uniqueness, I created this equation y=3.226^(x-5.5) (which is divided by 100) which has a margin of error of about 1 or 2 per most points. That means that for every bit, the amount of info multiplies by 32.26 every bit. There is not much more to be drawn besides that privacy focused browsers end up looking extremely unique and oddly giving away the most information.
