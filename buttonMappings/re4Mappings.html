You may be playing the Resident Evil 4 Remake and wanting to go back to the old version. I doubt the original pc port was kept up.
With my first button mapping posted, I will show you a great mouse and keyboard mapping (note: USE A FUCKING MOUSE. You need to middle click.)

Left Click to Interact with most things
Right Click to go into gun mode (is that what it's called)?
Minus to open map
Plus to open options
Command (ctrl (I'm on mac)) to open inventory
While it is annoying, to go back through any menu, right click. You know what, fuck it. Right click it, put | and put `Alt` for menus.
I might change it, but z to skip cutscenes and command Ashley. I haven't made it to that part yet.
You don't have to use home for the game, but you have use tab or space.
Up, Down, Left, Right, a believe for menus.
We use q, if you want to use knife with it you can, but I use it to shake off zombies.
Cursor to aim.
WASD to move.
Middle Click to pull out knife and Left Click while holding it to stab.
Shift to run.
